Mathematical Proof



If 2x + 2y = 2z, then x=y given that x,y and z are positive integers.


Given :


Only those tiles get merged that add up to form a number that is nth power of 2
Tiles begin from number 2 and x, y and z are greater than 1.

Proof :


Consider two tiles be 2x & 2y where x,y>=1. As sum 2x and 2y is divisible by 2, so consider z such that
2x + 2y = 2z

and n such that,
n=z-x
2x(1+2(y-x))=2z
(1 + 2(y-x))=2(z-x)
(1+ 2(y-x))=2n

R.H.S is a power of 2 with n>=1 as power z is sum of powers of x and y so, z-x >= 1.
Therefore, for L.H.S as a power of 2, 2(y-x) must be odd as 1 is added to it to make it even which can be only possible if y-x=0 as 20=1.
Hence, y-x=0
x=y.
Hence, proved.
